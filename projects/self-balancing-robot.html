Self-Balancing
Desk Robot.

A two-wheel desk companion designed to stay upright using LQR control and Kalman sensor fusion — my A-Level Computer Science project. This page tracks the live build: mechanical assembly first, then firmware, control theory, voice interaction, sensing and docking.
